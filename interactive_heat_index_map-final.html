About This Map

Shows U.S. heat stress risk using temperature and humidity.

Data is collected from National Centers for Environmental Information (NCEI), and shows the data from July, 2024.

Use sliders to adjust blur, opacity, size. Toggle categories from legend.

How to make use of this map?

- For a general heat distribution of the US, make the shapes bigger and blur.

- For a localized data, tap on the shapes to get a specific data.

- You can protect your workers by this classifications, for example, if your work location is in a dangerous zone, being outside over 10 minutes could lead to heat stroke.

- For newcomers, you can choose locations wisely based on real scientific data.

For more information please refer to https://github.com/nemuulen/heat_stress_data_visualization.
